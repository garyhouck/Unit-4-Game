Crystal Collector Game

Instructions:

You will be given a random number at the start of the game

There are four buttons below. By clicking on a button you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. You lose the game if your total score goes over the random number.

The value of each button is hidden from you until you click them.

Each time the game starts, the game will change the value of the buttons.
